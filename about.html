About Me



I am a born Australian, half German aspiring model, hobby photographer, and creative where I express myself in multiple forms of media. I spent 8 years of my life playing violin in various quartets, ensembles and an orchestra. All I want is to entertain and enchant those that spend time around me.


I am someone who wishes their body to be used as a canvas for artists to display their pride and work. I am a creative person that is inspired by nature and its many different forms. I am feminine. I am masculine. I am androgenous. I am unapologetically trans.
